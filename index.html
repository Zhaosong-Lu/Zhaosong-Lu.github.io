I joined the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at University of Minnesota in 2019. Prior to that, I was a faculty member in the Department of Mathematics at Simon Fraser University from 2006 to 2019, and was a Zeev Nehari Visiting Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Carnegie Mellon University during the academic year 2005-2006. I completed my PhD in Operations Research in the School of Industrial and Systems Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology in August 2005.

 

Research Interests
  • Theory and algorithms for deterministic and/or stochastic continuous optimization
  • Applications in finance, statistics, machine learning, image processing, engineering design, and decision-making under uncertainty
